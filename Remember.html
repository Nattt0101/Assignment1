Remember


Remember is the second Korean album by South Korean boy band Big Bang, released by YG Entertainment on November 5, 2008. Before release, demand for the album made it surpass 200,000 pre-orders. "Sunset Glow", originally by artist Lee Moon-sae, served as the title track for the album. During the 2008 KBS Music Festival, Big Bang performed the hit together with Lee Moon-sae. "Strong Baby", Seungri's solo track, was released as the second single. The music video was released January 1, 2009.
